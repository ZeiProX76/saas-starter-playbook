# The SaaS Starter Playbook

A practical, no-fluff guide for solo founders, indie hackers, and freelancers who want to build and launch a SaaS product. No theory. No hand-waving. Just the steps that actually work.

## The Problem

Building a SaaS product is expensive and slow. A typical MVP costs $5,000 to $50,000 if you hire a developer. If you build it yourself, you'll spend 3 to 6 months wiring up authentication, payments, email, database security, and a dozen other pieces of infrastructure before you write a single line of product code.

Most founders quit before they ship. Not because their idea was bad, but because the path from idea to live product was too long and too painful.

This playbook exists to shorten that path.
